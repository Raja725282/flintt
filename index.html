Frequently Asked Questions

The complete onboarding process typically takes 4-6 weeks, depending on your pace and availability. Each step is designed to be completed at your own speed.

You'll have access to dedicated onboarding specialists, live chat support, phone support, and our Slack community. Plus, regular check-in calls are scheduled throughout the process.

We recommend following the steps in order as each builds upon the previous one. However, some flexibility is possible depending on your specific situation.
