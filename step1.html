Welcome to Flint Directors
Step 1 FAQs

As a Flint Director, you own your business with your own ABN. You're not an employee but an independent business owner who has access to Flint's infrastructure, support, and platforms. This means you build equity, have more control over your operations, and benefit directly from your business growth.

While FlintEngine is essential for managing your business operations, FlintMedia is optional but highly recommended. Many successful Directors use both platforms together to maximize their lead generation and operational efficiency.

Step 1 typically takes 2-3 hours to complete thoroughly. This includes watching the welcome video, reading through all materials, downloading resources, and ensuring you understand the Flint model before moving to legal setup.
